SCHOOL GALA DAY-2

What Its All About

The school gala day at Divya Academy is one of the most exciting and eagerly awaited events of the year for both students and teachers alike. The school campus is filled with a carnival-like atmosphere, with colorful decorations and an array of stalls selling delicious food, drinks, and merchandise. The gala day is an excellent opportunity for students to showcase their creativity, talent, and team spirit. The event is organized and managed by the school administration and the students themselves, who take on various roles such as organizing stalls, managing games, and entertainment shows.
